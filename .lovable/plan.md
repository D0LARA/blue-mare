
# Blue Mare – Sea View Luxury Studio, Byala

## Website Plan

### Design System
- **Dark theme**: Deep navy (#0f1729) base, charcoal cards (#1a2332)
- **Accents**: Light blue (#7db8d4), muted sand (#c4a97d), sea-green (#5b9e8f)
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif, generous spacing, high contrast for accessibility
- **Motion**: Subtle fade-in on scroll, smooth hover transitions

### Structure (Single-page with anchor navigation)

**Sticky Navbar** – Logo "Blue Mare", anchor links to all sections, mobile hamburger menu, "Book Now" CTA button

**1. Hero Section** – Full-viewport with gradient overlay simulating sea/sunset atmosphere. Property name, tagline ("Your serene escape by the Black Sea"), two CTAs. Highlights row: Near Sea · Sleeps 2 · Free Wi-Fi · Sea-View Balcony

**2. About the Studio** – Brief intro paragraph + amenity cards with icons (Wi-Fi, AC, kitchenette, bathroom, sea view, workspace, self check-in)

**3. Gallery** – Responsive grid (8-10 placeholder images) with lightbox modal for full-screen viewing. Categorized placeholders for sea view, interior, balcony, etc.

**4. Why Stay Here** – Split layout with three columns: Comfort, Location, Experience – each with bullet points and trust-building copy

**5. Availability / Booking CTA** – Prominent card with date picker placeholders, inquiry button, notes on 2-night minimum and flexible cancellation

**6. Location** – Embedded map placeholder (coordinates: 42.869428, 27.897736), nearby POI list: beach, cafes, promenade, transport

**7. Reviews** – 5 testimonial cards with realistic guest quotes, names/initials, and star ratings

**8. FAQ** – Accordion with 6 common questions (parking, check-in, pets, cancellation, beach distance, Wi-Fi)

**9. Contact & Footer** – Inquiry form (name, email, dates, message), footer with contact placeholders, Facebook link (https://www.facebook.com/BlueMareByala), social icons, copyright

### SEO & Meta
- Proper semantic HTML (header, main, section, footer)
- Meta title, description, Open Graph tags in index.html
- Accessible forms with labels, focus states, ARIA attributes

### Components
- Navbar, Hero, AboutSection, Gallery (with lightbox), FeaturesSection, BookingCTA, LocationSection, TestimonialsSection, FAQSection, ContactForm, Footer
- Reusable: AmenityCard, TestimonialCard, FAQItem, HighlightBadge
